Touch Controls

Intuitive drag controls. Vertical for positioning, horizontal for fine adjustments. Tap to fire and shield.

Keyboard (iPad/Mac)

Keyboard controls. WASD for movement, K for missiles, H for shields. Perfect for extended play sessions.

FAQ

A run continues until you lose all cargo. You may play quick attempts or sustain much longer runs depending on how well you navigate hazards and use resources.

Space Train is available on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. It works offline and supports both touch controls on mobile and keyboard controls on iPad/Mac.

There are no pay-to-win mechanics. Ads will appear between runs and on the main screen; a one-time purchase removes them permanently.
