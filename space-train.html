Keyboard support

Monetization & fair play

Banner ads appear on menus and interstitials surface between runs with a cooldown. A one-time in-app purchase removes ads completely. No loot boxes, no pay-to-win, just fair runs.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No accounts required. Connect to Game Center only if you want leaderboard scoring.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Runs, achievements, and customization are fully offline. Ads require a connection, but gameplay does not.

Does the app support keyboards?

Absolutely. Use WASD to move, K to shoot, and H to shield on both macOS and iPadOS.
