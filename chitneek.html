Why Chitneek

Read without limits

Chitneek is designed to make your content accessible. Using your device's built-in text-to-speech, it reads your books and notes aloud so you can keep moving and stay productive.

Your Library, Anywhere

Import your own PDFs, MP3s, and ePUBs to listen on the go. Once downloaded, your files are available for offline listening — perfect for flights or commutes.

Natural TTS Voices

Powered by Apple's built-in text-to-speech engine, Chitneek reads your documents aloud with natural-sounding voices. Adjust speed and pitch to your preference.

Shared Groups

Don't read alone. Create shared groups to build a common library with friends, family, or study partners. Everyone contributes, everyone listens.

Web Libraries

Use the in-app browser to find and download PDFs or ePUBs from your favorite online sources directly into the app. Browse, tap, listen.

Features

Engineered for Listening

Listening, Your Way

Customizable playback, bookmarks, and a built-in sleep timer keep narration comfortable on every drive, walk, or focus session.

  • Adjustable speed & pitch. Fine-tune your listening experience.
  • Sleep timer. Fall asleep to your book without wasting battery.
  • Bookmarks. Mark your place and resume anytime.

Import & Manage

Capture documents in the format that works best for you. Build a personal listening queue from your own files, the web, or even YouTube.

  • Easy import. Add PDFs, EPUBs, MP3s from Files or Share Sheet.
  • YouTube support. Listen to YouTube content in-app.
  • In-app translation. Translate PDF books before listening.

Scan & Record

Use your camera to scan physical pages or record audio notes to save for later. Turn anything into listenable content.

  • Camera scanning. Photograph pages and convert to text.
  • Audio notes. Record voice memos directly in your library.

Browse & Download

Seamlessly browse audiobook and text catalogs directly within the app. Find free content from online libraries and add it to your collection in one tap.

  • Audiobook catalog. Browse and stream audio content.
  • Text book catalog. Access thousands of free ePUBs and PDFs.
  • Direct downloads. Content goes straight into your library.

Rich Audio Player

A full-featured audio player for your MP3s and MP4s. Beautiful playback controls with cover art, progress tracking, and chapter navigation.

  • Cover art display. Beautiful, album-style playback screen.
  • Progress tracking. Pick up right where you left off.
  • Background playback. Keep listening while using other apps.

Full interface and voice support for major languages. Many additional languages supported for voice playback.
