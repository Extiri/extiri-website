Privately read aloud

A private, on-device reader that can read aloud PDF and EPUB files on iPhone and iPad. Offline voices, organized library, and CarPlay support.

Why Chitneek

Simple, privacy-focused listening

Chitneek turns PDFs, EPUBs, notes, and web content into audio — all processed on your device.

Private & Offline

Files stay on your device for reliable, private listening without internet.

Organized Library

Use named groups to keep content tidy and easy to find.

Natural Voices

Uses Apple’s built‑in iOS voices for a natural experience.

Simple by Design

No accounts, no cloud — just add, tap, and listen.

Listening, Your Way

Customizable playback, CarPlay support, and clear audio keep narration comfortable on every drive, walk, or focus session.

  • • Customizable playback. Adjust speech rate and pitch.
  • • CarPlay support. Distraction‑free listening on the road.
  • • Clear audio. High‑quality, on-device voices.

Import and Discover

Capture documents in the format that works best for you and keep building a personal listening queue.

  • • Easy import. Add PDFs, EPUBs from Files or share sheet.
  • • Text → PDF. Paste text or save a web page.
  • • Discovery. Find books from Project Gutenberg.
