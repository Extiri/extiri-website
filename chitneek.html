FAQ

Is Chitneek paid?

Yes, but there is a 7-day free trial available for both of the subscription plans - monthly and yearly.

Does Chitneek work offline?

Yes. Files and pasted text are processed on‑device using iOS voices. Saving a web page to a PDF works offline after you save it.

Which voices does it use?

Chitneek uses Apple’s built‑in voices, so output sounds natural and works system‑wide.

How do I import content?

Add PDFs from Files or share into Chitneek from other apps. You can also paste text or save a web page to a PDF to listen later.

Is there a subscription?

Yes. A subscription is required to access all features.
