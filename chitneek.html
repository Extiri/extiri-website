FAQ

What can Chitneek read?

PDFs, websites, and plain text. Just add, paste, or enter a link.

Can I use Chitneek in my car?

Yes! Chitneek supports CarPlay for safe, hands-free listening.

Does it work offline?

Yes, for files and pasted text. Website reading requires an internet connection.
