About Extiri

Purposeful software for focused people.

Extiri is an independent studio led by Wiktor Wójcik. I build macOS, iOS, and web tools that streamline everyday work, respect your attention, and keep creative momentum high.

  • 2019 Studio founded
  • 6+ Products prototyped
  • Countless Workflows refined

What you’ll find here

The blog captures the experiments, systems, and lessons gathered while building Extiri’s products.

  • Deep dives into macOS engineering and design decisions.
  • Guides for automating daily workflows and knowledge capture.
  • Security-minded takes on keeping data and teams safe.
  • Occasional studio notes from behind the scenes.

How I ship

Every Extiri project is measured against a few principles:

  • Calm interfaces that stay out of your way.
  • Offline-first whenever privacy or speed demands it.
  • Composable features so you can adapt tools to your flow.

Current focus

I'm actively evolving CodeMenu, expanding the Extiri app catalog, and sharing the patterns that make these tools resilient.

If you enjoy building thoughtful software—or using it—this blog is for you.
