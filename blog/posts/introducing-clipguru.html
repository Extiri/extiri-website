Introducing ClipGuru — a lightweight clipboard manager

October 18, 2025 • Wiktor Wójcik • #extiri #app-showcase

ClipGuru is a small, mobile-first clipboard manager built for iPhone and iPad. It solves a simple problem: when you copy something important on your device, you should be able to save it and find it again. Add clips quickly by tapping Paste inside the app or by typing a new clip — saved items are then easy to search and reuse. The macOS app complements the mobile experience for when you’re on a desktop.

We’ve all been there — on your phone or tablet you copy a quick link or quote, then later it’s gone because you copied something else. ClipGuru gives you a simple place to save those items: tap Paste in the app (or enter the content) to add it to your ClipGuru history. Once saved, clips are searchable and ready to reuse across your devices.

Who is ClipGuru for?

ClipGuru is for anyone who copies things a lot: writers, developers, designers, students, researchers, and people who simply like to keep small pieces of information handy.

  • Researching an article: copy quotes, links, facts, and find them later without opening dozens of tabs.
  • Design work: copy screenshots or small assets to reuse in presentations and mockups.
  • Multi-device workflow: copy on iPad, paste on Mac, continue your work uninterrupted.

What makes ClipGuru different?

  • Lightweight and focused: it does one job well — store and retrieve clipboard items.
  • Fast search: find past clips by keyword in seconds.
  • Multi-device friendly: saved clips sync across your devices via iCloud — add a clip on your iPhone or iPad and it will appear on your other devices (macOS included) shortly after.
  • Special keyboard: thanks to a custom keyboard you can access snippets anywhere you need.
  • Quick review: review your snippets and decide whether you want to keep or delete them without distractions

Find snippets quicker

Ever copied five different things in a row and needed the third one? ClipGuru helps you avoid clipboard roulette: paste items into the app as you go, then search by keyword to find them instantly. Results are fast and support simple filters so you can narrow results quickly.

Keep your favorites close

Some clips matter more than others — a signature line, a frequently shared link, or a small image you reuse. Pin those items to keep them at the top of your list so they’re one tap away when you need them.

Lightweight by design

ClipGuru intentionally avoids feature bloat. No complex tagging systems or heavy UIs — just a clean history, favorites, and fast search. It’s designed to help without getting in the way.

Room to grow

The initial release focuses on the essentials. We’ll add features based on feedback, but the core promise remains: a small, reliable place to save and find the clips you care about.

ClipGuru offers a simple, no-fuss approach to clipboard management. If you often copy things and want a reliable way to save and reuse them across devices, ClipGuru is built for that. Try it on your phone or tablet and see how it fits into your workflow.

We’re keeping the initial release simple and iterating based on feedback. If you have ideas or workflows you’d love ClipGuru to support, tell us — we’re listening.

ClipGuru is available on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Try it and let your clipboard finally work for you.

For now, give it a try on your phone or tablet — see how it fits into your mobile workflow and let your clips work for you. ClipGuru stores text, links, and images that you add. Paste into the app (tap Paste) on iPhone or iPad to add a clip; saved clips sync via iCloud and will be available on your other devices, including the macOS companion app.

Because honestly, we’ve all got better things to do than hunt for that thing we copied five minutes ago.
