Space Train

Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 — Effective 02 Oct 2025

Your privacy matters. This document explains how Space Train uses data, which partners are involved, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

Space Train does not collect personal information directly. The game integrates Google AdMob to serve ads, which may automatically collect:

  • Device information such as model, operating system, and identifiers
  • IP address
  • Engagement data about your interactions with ads

AdMob processes this information under Google’s privacy policies. Learn more at policies.google.com/privacy.

2. How we use information

We rely on AdMob to keep the game free to play and to show relevant ads. The data collected is used solely to deliver and improve advertising. We do not sell or otherwise use this data for additional purposes.

3. Third-party services

Space Train partners with Google AdMob. Google determines how advertising data is processed. Review their policies for additional details:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob & Advertising

4. Children’s privacy

Space Train is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or under the age defined by local law). Ads are provided by AdMob with content guidelines for general audiences. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us and we will take action to remove it.

5. Data security

Space Train does not store personal data on Extiri servers. AdMob manages any collected information in line with industry security standards.

6. Your choices

You can manage ad preferences through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking or Settings > Google > Ads

Google also provides controls for personalized ads at support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product or legal changes. The updated policy will be posted here with a new effective date. Continuing to use the game after updates means you accept the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or our privacy practices, reach out to us:

  • Email: wiktor.wojcik@extiri.com
  • Support: support@extiri.com
