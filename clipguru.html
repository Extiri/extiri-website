FAQ

Is ClipGuru free?

ClipGuru has a free version that saves up to 4 clips. Pro features like unlimited history, iCloud sync, and tags are available with a one-time purchase.

Which platforms does ClipGuru support?

ClipGuru is available on iOS, iPadOS, and macOS through the App Store.

How does the keyboard extension work?

Install the ClipGuru keyboard to access your clips from any app. You can insert saved text, links, and snippets without switching between applications.

Can I sync my clips across devices?

Yes, with ClipGuru Pro you get iCloud sync to keep your clips synchronized across all your Apple devices.

What types of content can I save?

ClipGuru can save text, links, and images. You can also use the share extension to save content directly from Safari and other apps.
