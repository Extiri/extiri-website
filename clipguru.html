Why ClipGuru

Everything You Copy, Captured

Save text, links, and images with one tap. ClipGuru securely keeps your history ready whenever you need it.

Quick Save

Save text, links, and images instantly. A simple button at the bottom allows you to quickly add the item currently in your clipboard to ClipGuru.

Smart Search

Find any clip instantly. Search through all your saved content with built-in semantic understanding.

Custom Keyboard

Access your clips from any app seamlessly. The native ClipGuru keyboard extension lets you drop saved text right into your workflow.

Beautiful Design

Native iOS design tailored with a sophisticated dark mode support. It feels right at home on your iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Your Clipboard, Organized

An uncluttered, intuitive interface designed to help you quickly review and manage everything you've saved.

  • Rich previews. See content at a glance before pasting.
  • Tags. Keep important snippets organized and easy to access.

No Context Switching

The ClipGuru keyboard extension lets you retrieve and insert content from anywhere — no need to juggle between applications.

  • Instant access. Pull up the keyboard in Messages, Notes, or Mail.
  • Type faster. Insert snippets, links, and common replies with a single tap.
  • Stay in the flow. Keep your focus right where you are working.

Share from Anywhere

Use the native iOS share sheet to instantly save web pages, quotes, or images directly to ClipGuru without opening the app.

  • Share extension. Direct integration with Safari, Photos, and more.
  • Collect everything. Rapidly gather research, URLs, and inspiration.

Quick Review

Easily maintain a focused history. Open Quick Review at any time to go through your clips full-screen one by one and quickly decide whether to keep or remove each item.

Semantic Search

Find snippets based on meaning, not just exact keywords.

Pro Features

Upgrade to ClipGuru Pro with a one-time purchase to unlock unlimited clip history, iCloud sync, and organizational features.

  • Unlimited history. Save an unlimited number of clips without restrictions.
  • iCloud sync. Securely share your clipboard across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
  • Organize with tags. Keep your important snippets categorized and easy to find.
